About Us

Splash Water Park Abuja is a premier destination for family fun and entertainment in the heart of Nigeria's capital. The park offers thrilling slides, a lazy river, and a wave pool, perfect for guests of all ages. With live entertainment and diverse dining options, Splash Water Park ensures a memorable experience for all visitors.
