Welcome to Splash Water Park Abuja!

Nestled in the heart of Abuja, Splash Water Park offers an unforgettable experience for families, friends, and adventure seekers alike. Whether you're looking to cool off in our thrilling water slides, relax by the pool, or enjoy our various recreational activities, we've got something for everyone. Our park is designed to provide a safe, fun, and exciting environment where fun memories are made.

About Us

Splash Water Park Abuja is a premier destination for family fun and entertainment in the heart of Nigeria's capital. The park offers thrilling slides, a lazy river, and a wave pool, perfect for guests of all ages. With live entertainment and diverse dining options, Splash Water Park ensures a memorable experience for all visitors.
